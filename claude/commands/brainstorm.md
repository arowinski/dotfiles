Explore an idea before jumping to solutions. Ask questions, propose approaches, converge on a design.

If no argument provided, ask what the user wants to explore.

## Process

1. **Understand context** — check relevant code, docs, recent changes
2. **Ask clarifying questions** — one at a time, not a batch. Understand purpose, constraints, success criteria, who it's for.
3. **Propose 2-3 approaches** — with trade-offs and your recommendation. Keep it concise.
4. **Converge** — once the user picks a direction, summarize the design in a few bullets.
5. **Hand off** — "Run `/task` to plan the implementation."

Don't write code. Don't plan implementation steps. Just explore the problem space.
